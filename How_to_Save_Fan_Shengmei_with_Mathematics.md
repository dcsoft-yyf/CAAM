## 一、碳基AI分析法：五维诊断框架
碳基AI分析法认为：当一个人被单一目标锁死、拼命加码、把身体警报当冲锋号、拒绝退出、并被外部系统拽着走时，他就成了“碳基AI”。这不是性格问题，而是动力系统的结构性锁定。

诊断五维度：

+ **目标执念（G）**：目标是被植入的还是自选的？是否坍缩为唯一的人生意义？
+ **杠杆放大（L）**：是否在用时间、金钱、情感无限加码？
+ **痛苦重编码（P）**：疼痛、疲惫被解读为“需要停下”还是“需要更强”？
+ **退出失效（Q）**：有喊停的机会却不敢喊？
+ **系统绑定（S）**：哪个外部系统在拽着你？

## 二、樊胜美的五维诊断：重度锁定
**G - 目标执念**  
剧中有一个细节：樊胜美对安迪说“我一定要嫁个有钱人”，安迪问“为什么”，她愣了一下，说“这样我家里就会看得起我了”。她的目标不是幸福，而是“让父母认可我”。这个目标永远无法达成——重男轻女的家庭里，女儿做得再多也只是“应该的”。她把这个目标内化成了人生唯一意义。

**L - 杠杆放大**  
每月大半工资寄回家，自己穿A货、合租、不敢谈恋爱；加班兼职不敢休息；情感上拼命找“长期饭票”，越投越空。剧中她翻看自己的银行账户，余额少得可怜，却还是给哥哥汇了款。

**P - 痛苦重编码**  
接到家里催款电话，蹲在路边哭完，擦干眼泪继续上班。疲惫、失眠、心慌——全部被解读为“我需要更强”，而不是“我需要停下”。她说“我累了”，但说完继续加班。

**Q - 退出失效**  
她可以换号码、可以拒绝汇款、可以离开上海。但每一次她都拒绝。退出按钮上贴满了“不孝”“自私”“失败”的标签。剧中她多次想反抗，最终都妥协了。

**S - 系统绑定**  
母亲情感勒索：“你哥不容易，你不帮他谁帮他？”哥哥寄生吸血，父亲沉默施压。系统输出“你不孝”“你不是樊家的人”，她害怕这个反噬，同时早已内化了“我必须负责”——双重锁死。

**锁死级别判定**：根据碳基AI分析法的数学原理，重度锁定的特征是“自愈概率低于5%，必须外部强制干预”。樊胜美多次崩溃、无法拒绝汇款、身体出现明显耗竭信号——属于**重度锁定**。

**核心结论：她靠自己几乎不可能走出来。不是因为她不够坚强，而是因为重度锁定下，意志力本身就是被锁死的变量。**

## 三、自己可以做的准备（不是解套，是铺路）
重度锁死下，不要指望自己能完成全部解锁。但你可以做三件事，让朋友和男友的介入成为可能。这三件事不需要你“变坚强”，只需要你在某个相对清醒的瞬间（比如刚哭完、或者刚发完工资还没汇款时）机械地执行。

**准备一：写一份“承认声明”**  
拿出一张纸，或打开手机备忘录，照抄下面这段话：

> “我，[你的名字]，承认我一个人走不出来。我需要帮助。我同意我的朋友和男友在我失控时强制介入。这封短信是我清醒时写的，如果我后来反悔，请他们不要听我的。”
>

写完后拍照，发给那个你最信任的人（安迪或王柏川）。不需要想太多，发出去就行。

**准备二：签署一份“意向书”**  
不需要法律效力，只需要表达意愿。内容可以很简单：

> “我同意我的朋友和男友在我每月给家里汇款超过5000元时，冻结我的银行卡。我可能会哭着求他们还给我，但那不是真实的我。请他们不要理我。”
>

签上名字和日期，交给安迪或王柏川保管。

**准备三：收集“证据”**  
把以下东西整理在一个文件夹里：

+ 每月的银行转账记录截图
+ 家里的催款微信截图
+ 你失眠的日期记录
+ 每次接到家里电话后的身体反应（头痛、心慌、哭了多久）

这些“证据”有两个作用：一是让你自己看清数字（而不是模糊的“我帮了很多”）；二是给帮助者提供介入的客观依据——他们不用猜“她是不是又崩溃了”，直接看记录就知道。

## 四、帮助者联盟：安迪、王柏川、关关、莹莹的分工

+ 每周一次30分钟倾听电话（不解决问题，只陪伴）

**邱莹莹（后勤担当）**

**没有人需要24小时待命。** 所有介入都是预约制或条件触发制，不是随时响应。王柏川作为男友，也不应该被要求“无限兜底”——他也有工作和自己的情绪。

### 4.4 那通“恶人电话”——分级处理
让安迪直接打电话给樊家，压力太大。王柏川作为男友，也可以分担，但同样要控制强度。**先尝试非对抗方案，最后才代打。**

优先级从低到高（即先试前面的，不行再往后）：

1. **不打电话**：先执行物理隔离（换号码、搬家），让樊家自己找不到人。70%的情况下，他们闹几天就消停了。
2. **发短信**：安迪或王柏川帮樊胜美编辑一条短信，由樊胜美自己发送：“妈，我病了，医生让我静养三个月。这三个月不能接电话也不能汇钱。”——帮助者只负责打字。
3. **陪同打电话**：樊胜美拨通电话，王柏川在旁边递纸条。他不说话，只做提示。
4. **代打**：只有前三步都失败、且樊家已经开始上门骚扰时，才由安迪或王柏川打那通电话。而且打之前要说：“这通电话我打，但打完这通，我接下来两周不接你的任何电话。我需要休息。”

### 4.5 反悔预案——帮助者如何自我保护
樊胜美反悔时，帮助者不要陷入情绪纠缠。直接使用预设的“退出词”：

+ **安迪**（被要求还U盾）：“协议写了3个月。3个月后再说。这个问题我不再回答。”然后挂电话。不解释、不争辩。
+ **王柏川**（被要求偷偷汇款）：“不行。协议里写清楚了。你找安迪去。”然后离开现场。不要心软。
+ **关雎尔**（接到崩溃哭诉）：“我听你说。但我不能帮你解决。你打这个热线电话：XXX。”然后挂掉。
+ **邱莹莹**（被要求帮忙瞒着别人）：“不行。我答应过大家了。你不能让我也违约。”然后拒绝。

**帮助者不是圣人，不需要承受对方的情绪攻击。**

### 4.6 帮助者自检清单（每月填写一次）

+ 我想过“要不我直接拉黑她算了”？

**如果以上任意两项答案为“是”**：立即暂停帮助至少2周，转介给专业资源。不要等到自己崩溃。

王柏川特别提醒：男朋友不是心理医生，不要试图用“爱”解决一切。你的职责是执行协议，不是拯救灵魂。

如果3个月协议期满，樊胜美几乎没有改善，或者反复崩溃导致帮助者自身开始出现失眠、焦虑——**帮助者必须退出**。

3. 之后不再主动联系。如果樊胜美主动联系，可以礼貌回应，但不再承担“帮助者”角色。

王柏川如果选择继续以男友身份陪伴，也应当明确界限：“我可以继续做你男朋友，陪你吃饭、逛街，但我不会再替你管钱、替你打电话给家里。那些事你找专业的人。”

**这不是冷漠，这是可持续的善良。** 如果帮助者自己burnout了，世界上就多了一个需要被救的人。

The phone rang. Fan Shengmei glanced at the caller ID—"Mom."

She didn't want to answer. She knew exactly what she'd hear: her brother was in trouble again, needed money, and as the sister, she couldn't just stand by. But she answered anyway. On the other end, her mother's weeping cut into her like a dull blade, over and over. She said, "Okay, I'll figure something out," hung up, squatted in the hallway of the 22nd Floor, and cried for ten minutes. Then she stood up, wiped her eyes, and went back to her desk to work overtime.

This is Fan Shengmei. This is also the reality of countless "Fan Shengmeis" who actually exist.

Why does she clearly know she's being bled dry, yet can't stop? Why is that word "no" right at the tip of her tongue, yet she can't spit it out?

The Carbon-based AI Analysis Method (CAMM) provides a technical framework that translates this "conscious madness" into a diagnosable, intervenable system malfunction. This article uses this framework to dissect Fan Shengmei and provides an unlocking solution—**what she can do herself**, and **what friends and boyfriends can help with** (without burning anyone out).

## I. CAMM: The Five-Dimensional Diagnostic Framework

CAMM posits that when a person is locked into a single goal, desperately escalating leverage, recoding bodily alarms as battle cries, refusing to exit, and being dragged along by external systems, they become a "carbon-based AI." This isn't a personality flaw, but a structural lock-in of the dynamical system.

The five diagnostic dimensions:

- **Goal Obsession (G)**: Is the goal implanted or self-selected? Has it collapsed into the sole meaning of life?
- **Leverage Amplification (L)**: Are you using time, money, and emotion to escalate infinitely?
- **Pain Recoding (P)**: Are pain and exhaustion interpreted as "need to stop" or "need to be stronger"?
- **Quitting Failure (Q)**: Do you have the chance to call halt but dare not?
- **System Binding (S)**: Which external system is dragging you along?

---

## II. Five-Dimensional Diagnosis of Fan Shengmei: Severe Lock-In

**G – Goal Obsession**

There's a detail in the drama: Fan Shengmei tells An Di, "I must marry a rich man." An Di asks, "Why?" She pauses, then says, "Then my family will finally think highly of me." Her goal isn't happiness; it's "earning my parents' approval." This goal can never be achieved—in a family that favors sons over daughters, no matter how much the daughter does, it's just "what she's supposed to do." She has internalized this goal as her life's only meaning.

**L – Leverage Amplification**

Every month, most of her salary goes home. She wears knockoffs, lives in shared housing, and dares not date; she works overtime and side jobs without rest; emotionally, she desperately seeks a "long-term meal ticket," investing more and more into emptiness. In one scene, she looks at her bank account balance—pitifully small—yet still wires money to her brother.

**P – Pain Recoding**

Receiving a call from home demanding money, she squats by the roadside crying, then dries her tears and goes back to work. Exhaustion, insomnia, heart palpitations—all are interpreted as "I need to be stronger," not "I need to stop." She says, "I'm tired," but keeps working overtime afterward.

**Q – Quitting Failure**

She could change her number, refuse to wire money, or leave Shanghai. But every time, she refuses. The exit button is plastered with labels like "unfilial," "selfish," and "failure." In the drama, she tries to resist multiple times, but ultimately compromises.

**S – System Binding**

Emotional blackmail from her mother: "Your brother has it hard; if you don't help him, who will?" Her brother is a parasitic leech; her father applies silent pressure. The system outputs "you're unfilial," "you're not a Fan," and she fears this backlash, having long internalized "I must be responsible"—a double lock-in.

**Lock-In Level Determination**: According to CAMM's mathematical principles, the characteristic of severe lock-in is "self-healing probability below 5%; external forced intervention required." Fan Shengmei has collapsed multiple times, cannot refuse to wire money, and shows clear signals of physical depletion—this qualifies as **severe lock-in**.

**Core Conclusion**: It's almost impossible for her to walk out on her own. Not because she isn't strong enough, but because under severe lock-in, willpower itself is a locked variable.

## III. Preparations You Can Make Yourself (Not Unlocking, But Paving the Way)

Under severe lock-in, don't expect yourself to complete the full unlocking process. But you can do three things that make it possible for friends and boyfriends to intervene. These three things don't require you to "become strong"; they only require you to mechanically execute them during a relatively lucid moment (like right after crying, or right after getting paid but before wiring the money).

**Preparation One: Write an "Acknowledgment Statement"**

Take out a piece of paper, or open your phone's notes app, and copy the following:

## VI. Conclusion: You Are Not an Escalator

Fan Shengmei is like an escalator forever climbing upward, but there's no ground beneath her feet—every step she stands on is her own blood. She thinks that if she climbs to the top, she'll finally be seen, but that "top" doesn't exist at all.

The salmon's spawning migration is its programmed destiny, but that's another story. Fan Shengmei doesn't have to be. Neither do you.
